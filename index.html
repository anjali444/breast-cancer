☰ Breast Cancer Awareness

Breast Cancer affects about 1 in 8 women in a lifetime. You might know a loved one who has breast cancer or is a survivor. Perhaps you are curious to know what is breast cancer and how to support the fight against it. In "Breast Cancer Awareness," you can discover risk factors and different types of breast cancer. Additionally, there are available resources listed for breast cancer patients.
